ApexPulse — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. Overview

ApexPulse ("the App") is a desktop companion application for Apex Legends. This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how it is stored, and how it is used. The short version: all your data stays on your computer. We do not operate servers, and we do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or telemetry
  • We do not track your browsing or app usage behavior
  • We do not collect your IP address, hardware identifiers, or device fingerprints
  • We do not store any data on remote servers
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party

4. Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following external services during normal use:

4.1 Apex Legends Status API (apexlegendsapi.com)

When you provide an API key, the App sends requests to this API containing your EA/Origin username to retrieve profile statistics, map rotations, and server status. This is subject to the Apex Legends Status API's own privacy policy. We do not control what data that service logs.

4.2 Steam OpenID

If you choose to sign in with Steam, the App initiates a standard OpenID authentication flow with Valve's servers. Your Steam ID, display name, and avatar URL are returned and stored locally. No data is sent to us.

4.3 Discord OAuth

If you choose to sign in with Discord, the App initiates a standard OAuth2 flow with Discord's servers. Your Discord ID, username, and avatar URL are returned and stored locally. No data is sent to us.

4.4 Overwolf Platform

The App runs on the Overwolf Electron platform, which may collect its own telemetry and display ads subject to Overwolf's privacy policy and CMP (Consent Management Platform) settings. Overwolf's data practices are separate from ours — please review Overwolf's Privacy Policy for details.

4.5 Google Fonts

The App loads the Inter and JetBrains Mono fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Screen Capture

The App uses screen capture to detect Apex Pack openings for the heirloom pack tracker. Captured frames are processed locally using OCR (Tesseract.js) and are never stored, transmitted, or recorded. The capture region is limited to a small area of the game screen and only runs when Apex Legends is active and outside of a match.

6. Lobby Intel

During a match, the App receives a roster of player names in the current lobby via the Overwolf GEP. It may look up publicly available statistics for these players using the Apex Legends Status API. This data is held in memory only for the duration of the match and is not stored permanently.

7. Data Storage and Retention

All persistent data is stored locally in your operating system's user data directory:

  • Windows: %APPDATA%\apexpulse\

Data is retained until you uninstall the App or manually delete the data directory. The App does not automatically delete your data.

8. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: Your data is in the files listed above — you can open and inspect them at any time
  • Deletion: Uninstall the App or delete the data directory to remove all data
  • Export: The App provides a data export feature in Settings (JSON format)
  • Portability: You can copy the data directory to another machine

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we operate no servers, we have no mechanism to identify or remove such data remotely.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
